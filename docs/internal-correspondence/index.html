Office · Internal Correspondence

Internal Correspondence office dashboard for signatures, memos, letters, and controlled templates.

This route serves as the bounded internal correspondence namespace inside the Office layer of the website architecture. It establishes the primary dashboard surface for signature systems, memo structures, letter structures, and template-grade communication pages without mixing internal office assets into the public website or future product platform layers.

Primary office route families

Layer boundary premise

Public website logic remains separated from Office rendering. The Office layer remains distinct from the future ICOS product layer and is prepared for later protected-access, employee-number-based entry, export-grade layouts, and role-bounded communication systems without requiring structural rework.

Platform compatibility track

This Office layer is being structured from the start for future theme compatibility, language awareness, and dashboard-grade environment controls so the internal platform can evolve without re-architecture.

Department-aware routing track

Future employee-number-based access will identify operator scope by department and office so the dashboard surface can later resolve the correct navigation, task environment, and operational visibility for each user context.
